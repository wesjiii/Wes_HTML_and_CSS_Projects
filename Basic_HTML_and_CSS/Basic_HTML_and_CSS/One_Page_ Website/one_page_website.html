About Cars-R-Us
Cars-R-Us started in 1952 and was founded by Wes Johnson. He was an absolute genius and started selling cars at the age of 4. His family realized this and instead of having him go the normal route of school, they shipped him to Los Angeles where he would sell cars professionally at a Ferrari dealership. Eventually he got the bright idea to start his own dealership, which eventually grew into Cars-R-Us.
Cars-R-Us has sold to many notable names such a Leonardo DiCaprio, LeBron James, Ryan Gosling, Bill Gates, Dave Chapelle and more.
